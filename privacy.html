HighFive Privacy Policy

Your data stays on your device. Always.

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

HighFive does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

HighFive is a child behavior tracking app developed by Corey Battle. It is designed to help parents and caregivers log praise and correction events for their children. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

The short version: your data never leaves your device. There is no server, no cloud sync, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

Data Storage

All data created within HighFive is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This means your information exists only on the device where the app is installed. The data stored includes:

  • Child names you enter
  • Emoji selections associated with children
  • Praise and correction events you log
  • App settings and preferences

None of this data is ever transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

What We Do NOT Collect

HighFive does not collect any personal information. Specifically, the app does not collect or access:

Personal data Email addresses Location data Device identifiers Usage analytics Crash reports Cookies IP addresses

No Account Required

HighFive does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app. You can start using it immediately after installation.

No Analytics or Tracking

The app does not include any analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, or third-party monitoring tools. Your usage of the app is entirely private and unmonitored.

No Third-Party Sharing

Since HighFive does not collect any data, there is no data to share. We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any information with third parties.

Data Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can delete all app data by:

  • Clearing the app's data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app from your device

No residual data will remain on any server, because no data was ever sent to one.

Children's Privacy

HighFive is designed for use by parents and caregivers. While the app stores child names and behavioral data, this information is kept entirely on-device and is never collected, transmitted, or accessible to the developer or any third party.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. Since the app does not collect any contact information, users are encouraged to check this page periodically.
